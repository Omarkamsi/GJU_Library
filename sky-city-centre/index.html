What We Treat

Specialized care across the full recovery journey.

Every treatment plan begins with a thorough clinical assessment and a conversation about what matters most to you. Your goals guide the program.

Spinal Rehabilitation

Evidence-based care for chronic back pain, disc issues, postural imbalances, and spinal injury recovery.

Post-Surgical Recovery

Structured rehabilitation after orthopedic, spinal, or joint surgery — returning you safely to full function.

Sports & Orthopedic Injury

From weekend warriors to competitive athletes — sprains, strains, ligament tears, tendinopathies, and return-to-play programs.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Stroke recovery, Bell's palsy (facial nerve), multiple sclerosis, Parkinson's, and neurological conditions — rebuilding movement and independence.

Pain Management

Manual therapy, therapeutic modalities, and movement-based treatment for chronic and acute pain relief.

Mobility & Gait Restoration

From wheelchair to walking — progressive programs that rebuild strength, balance, and everyday mobility.

A Patient's Story
